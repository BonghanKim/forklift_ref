Assumptions on parameters, timing, etc
Class Controller
Assumes, that all computations are completed before reception of the next synchronization signal. (Otherwise control iterations may be skipped).
Class navserverConnection

Assumes, that the state and data from encoders are sent in the same TCP packet.

Assumes, that the delays in transmission of the TCP packets are small (less than 20 ms).

Assumes, that no more than one message can be available in the reception buffer at the same time.

Member qpProblem::qpOASES (const swParameters &, double &)

Control gains are > tolerance (positive and not too small).

Control inputs never have -/+Inf bounds.

Class stateContainer
Assumes that the expected steering angle is always reached. Hence, the steering angle reported by sensors is ignored. If it is not ignored the performance of the controller degrades due to a delay in sensor reports. The duration of the delay is about 3 sampling periods (3*60 ms). Computation of an error with 180ms delay does not make any improvement, most likely due to the work of the onboard controller.
